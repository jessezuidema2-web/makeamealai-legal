Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Make a Meal AI ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, name, and profile information you provide during registration (gender, height, weight, fitness goals, dietary preferences).
  • Usage data: Ingredient scans, recipes generated, meals logged, workout sessions, water intake, and weight logs.
  • Photos: Images you take or select for ingredient scanning. These are processed by AI services and stored securely.
  • Health data: If you enable health sync, we read/write step count, weight, nutrition, and workout data to Apple Health or Google Health Connect. This data stays on your device and in your health app.
  • Device data: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized recipe suggestions based on your ingredients and dietary preferences.
  • To track your nutrition, workouts, and fitness progress.
  • To manage your subscription and account.
  • To improve the app and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (hosted in EU/US).
  • Google Gemini & OpenAI: AI-powered ingredient analysis and recipe generation. Photos are sent to these services for processing but are not stored by them.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management.
  • Open Food Facts & FatSecret: Nutritional data lookup.
  • Google Places: Restaurant finder feature.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security enabled on every table. API keys are stored server-side and never exposed to the client. All communication uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes all your data including scans, meals, workouts, and stored images.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app.
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete your entire account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Contact us to request an export of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Make a Meal AI is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@makeamealai.com.
